## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
2. Halve potatoes. Toss with half the olive oil, salt, pepper. Spread on a large baking tray.
3. Roast potatoes for 15 minutes.
4. Trim green beans. Halve cherry tomatoes.
5. Add green beans, tomatoes, and minced garlic to the tray around the potatoes.
6. Place salmon fillets on top. Drizzle with remaining olive oil, squeeze lemon, sprinkle dill.
7. Roast for another 15 minutes until salmon is cooked through.

## Notes

- Can swap salmon for cod or sea bass (adjust protein slightly)
- Add capers or olives for extra Mediterranean flavor
