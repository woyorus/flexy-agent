A simple sheet-pan meal — salmon, crispy potatoes, and green beans all roasted together. Lemon and dill keep it fresh. Everything on one tray, minimal cleanup.

### Steps

1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
2. Halve the potatoes. Toss with half the olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a large baking tray.
3. Roast the potatoes for 15 minutes.
4. Trim the green beans. Halve the cherry tomatoes.
5. Add green beans, tomatoes, and minced garlic to the tray around the potatoes.
6. Place salmon fillets on top. Drizzle with remaining olive oil, squeeze lemon, sprinkle dill.
7. Roast for another 15 minutes until salmon is cooked through.

### Storage

Fridge: 3 days in airtight container. Not freezable.
Reheat: oven at 180°C for 8-10 min, covered with foil.

### Notes

- Can swap salmon for cod or sea bass (adjust protein slightly)
- Add capers or olives for extra Mediterranean flavor
