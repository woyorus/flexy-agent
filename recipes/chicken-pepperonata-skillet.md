A Mediterranean-style skillet chicken with sweet roasted peppers and tomatoes, served over rice. Bold, simple flavors — oregano, garlic, good olive oil.

### Chicken Pepperonata

1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Cook chicken until golden, about 5-6 minutes. Remove and set aside.
4. In the same skillet, cook sliced onion and peppers until softened, ~5 min.
5. Add minced garlic, cook 30 seconds.
6. Add canned tomatoes, stir, bring to simmer.
7. Return chicken. Cook together 5 minutes until sauce thickens slightly.

### Rice

Cook basmati rice according to package directions.

### Storage

Fridge: 3 days in airtight container. Freezable.
Reheat: pan on medium heat, 3-4 min. Add a splash of water if dry.

### Notes

- Can swap chicken for turkey breast (similar macros)
- Works well with zucchini instead of peppers
