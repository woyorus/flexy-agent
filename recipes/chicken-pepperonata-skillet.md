## Steps

1. Cook rice according to package directions. Set aside.
2. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. Season with salt, pepper, oregano.
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Cook chicken until golden, about 5-6 minutes. Remove and set aside.
5. In same skillet, cook sliced onion and peppers until softened, ~5 min.
6. Add minced garlic, cook 30 seconds.
7. Add canned tomatoes, stir, bring to simmer.
8. Return chicken. Cook together 5 minutes.
9. Serve over rice.

## Notes

- Can swap chicken for turkey breast (similar macros)
- Works well with zucchini instead of peppers
