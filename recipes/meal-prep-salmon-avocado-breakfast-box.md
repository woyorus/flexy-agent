## Steps

1. Finely dice the bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, and red onion. Reserve about one-third of the vegetables for the omelette. Mash the avocado with the lemon juice, the remaining vegetables, half the salt, and a little black pepper until chunky like a quick guacamole. Portion into 3 small airtight containers, pressing the surface smooth.
2. Whisk the eggs, liquid egg whites, oat milk, the reserved vegetables, the remaining salt, and the rest of the black pepper. Cook in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat as one thick omelette or soft scramble until just set. Cool and divide into 3 portions.
3. In a small pot, cook the rolled oats with 720 ml water until creamy, about 5 minutes. Stir in the chia seeds for the last minute. Divide into 3 containers.
4. Divide the Greek yogurt into 3 cups. Top each with walnuts and a pinch of cinnamon.
5. Pack the bread and smoked salmon separately so they stay fresh. When serving, reheat the oats and egg, toast the bread, spread on the avocado mixture, add the smoked salmon, and eat with the oatmeal and yogurt cup.

## Notes

This is built for 3 servings. For the best avocado color and texture, press plastic wrap or a reusable wrap directly onto the surface of each avocado portion before sealing.
