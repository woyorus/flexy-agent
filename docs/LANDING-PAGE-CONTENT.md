## Flexibility is the method

### The plan has to bend, or it breaks.

This system does not try to make weight loss work by adding more rules.

It does not demonize foods.
It does not rely on rigid meal plans.
It does not pretend cravings or social eating should disappear.

Instead:

* fun foods are part of the system
* flexibility is expected, not treated as failure
* one imperfect meal does not ruin the week
* consistency matters more than perfection

## Walk more. Suffer less.

### A more livable way to create the deficit

Most diets try to force progress mainly by making food smaller and life more miserable.

This system takes a different approach.

Keep food intake as high and livable as possible, then create more of the deficit through low-intensity activity like walking.

Walking is simple.
Easy to repeat.
Easy to recover with.
Easy to keep doing.
