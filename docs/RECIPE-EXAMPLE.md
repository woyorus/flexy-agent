Mushroom Pork with Potato
Dinner + Macro Goal

Meal: Dinner
Target (per meal): 812 kcal, 50P / 44F / 55C (ranges: 780–860 kcal, 47–55P, 40–50F, 48–62C)

⸻

Structure

One-pan main: Sheet-pan paprika pork + crispy roasted potatoes + garlicky mushrooms
(All roasted together on one tray; super simple.)

⸻

Recipe (meal prep: 3 servings)

Ingredients (for 3 servings)
	•	Pork tenderloin: 600 g (whole piece)
	•	Potatoes (white or red): 990 g (cut into 2–3 cm chunks)
	•	Mushrooms: 450 g (halved/quartered)
	•	Olive oil: 90 g (≈ 6 Tbsp)
	•	Almonds: 45 g (roughly chopped) (optional but helps hit fats/calories)
	•	Garlic: 12 g (3–4 cloves), minced
	•	Smoked paprika: 6 g (≈ 2 tsp)
	•	Dried oregano or rosemary: 3 g (≈ 1 tsp)
	•	Salt: 10 g total (adjust to taste)
	•	Black pepper
	•	Lemon: 1 (zest + wedges) (optional but great)

Steps
	1.	Heat oven to 220°C (or 200°C fan). Line a large sheet pan with baking paper.
	2.	Potatoes first (crisp step): Toss potatoes with 45 g olive oil, half the salt, pepper, half the paprika, and oregano/rosemary. Spread out well.
Roast 25 minutes, flipping once halfway.
	3.	Season pork: Rub tenderloin with 15 g olive oil, remaining paprika, salt, pepper, and a little lemon zest (optional).
	4.	Add pork + mushrooms: Toss mushrooms with 30 g olive oil + garlic + pepper.
Push potatoes to one side, add pork and mushrooms to the tray.
	5.	Finish roast: Roast another 15–18 minutes (until pork is 63°C internal or just cooked through).
Rest pork 5–8 minutes, then slice.
	6.	Serve: Divide potatoes + mushrooms + pork. Top each serving with 15 g almonds and lemon wedges.

Time + Equipment
	•	Time: ~45 minutes total (10 min prep, ~35 min oven)
	•	Equipment: sheet pan, bowl, knife, (thermometer optional but helpful)

⸻

“A Few Options” (same base, easy swaps)

Pick one of these flavor tweaks without changing the workflow:
	1.	Mustard-herb pork: swap paprika → 15 g Dijon + extra rosemary + lemon.
	2.	Garlic-paprika “asado” vibe: add 1 tsp cumin + pinch chili, finish with sherry vinegar.
	3.	Mushroom upgrade: add 150 g onion wedges to the mushrooms (same tray, same timing).

⸻

Meal Prep Block
	•	Servings: 3
	•	Storage: Cool, then fridge in airtight containers up to 3 days.
	•	Reheat:
	•	Best: oven/air fryer 200°C 8–12 min to re-crisp potatoes
	•	Fast: microwave 2–3 min, then quick pan/air fryer 3–5 min
	•	Make it faster: Use baby potatoes (halve) and pre-sliced mushrooms; skip lining the tray (just oil it).
